How We Deliver

A Predictable Path from
Problem to Production

No black boxes. Every engagement follows a structured path with clear decisions, milestones, and delivery checkpoints.

01

Discovery & Data Audit

Week 1

We interview stakeholders, map your current workflow, identify bottlenecks, and assess constraints (business, technical, and regulatory).

Deliverables: Bottleneck map, feasibility assessment, risk register, preliminary solution path
02

Solution Design & Prototyping

Week 2–3

We choose the simplest solution that can create measurable impact, then deliver a working prototype aligned to your workflow.

Deliverables: Solution blueprint, build plan, working prototype, success metrics baseline
03

Build & Evaluate

Week 4–7

We build production-ready workflows, integrations, and interfaces. Weekly demos keep scope tight and decisions fast.

Deliverables: Production-ready system, validation results, security checks, launch checklist
04

Deploy & Launch

Week 8

We launch in stages, instrument the system, and train your team so the solution is usable and maintainable on day one.

Deliverables: Deployed system, dashboards, handoff docs, team training session
05

Monitor, Retrain & Improve

Ongoing

Post-launch, we track outcomes, iterate on weak spots, and improve performance. Optional ongoing support or full handoff to your team.

Deliverables: Outcome reviews, improvement backlog, system updates, governance documentation

Security Basics

Security by Default,
Compliance When Required

Most projects need strong security fundamentals first. In regulated environments, we add deeper controls from day one.

We don't train foundation models on your data. Your data stays isolated and is retained only per agreement.

HIPAA Alignment (When Applicable)

For healthcare use cases, we design infrastructure with HIPAA requirements in mind—encryption, access controls, and audit logging.

SOC 2-Informed Practices

We use SOC 2-aligned controls such as encrypted storage, least-privilege access, and incident response procedures.

Data Governance

Data lineage tracking, retention policies, and anonymization workflows with documentation to support audits when required.

AI Risk Management

Model evaluation, guardrails, and human review for high-impact decisions, guided by the NIST AI Risk Management Framework.

Questions about our security practices? Reach out—we're happy to discuss in detail.
