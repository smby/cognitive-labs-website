How We Deliver

A Predictable Path from
Problem to Production

No black boxes. Every engagement follows a structured process with defined deliverables at each stage.

01

Discovery & Data Audit

Week 1

We interview stakeholders, audit your data sources, assess quality, and map constraints (regulatory, technical, organizational).

Deliverables: Data quality report, feasibility assessment, risk register, preliminary architecture
02

Solution Design & Prototyping

Week 2–3

We select the right model strategy (open-source vs. hosted, cost vs. latency tradeoffs), build the data pipeline, and deliver a working proof-of-concept.

Deliverables: Architecture doc, model selection rationale, working prototype, evaluation benchmarks
03

Build & Evaluate

Week 4–7

Production engineering: hardened pipelines, API endpoints, security controls, load testing. Weekly demos ensure alignment. Models are evaluated against domain-specific metrics.

Deliverables: Production-ready system, evaluation report, security review, load test results
04

Deploy & Launch

Week 8

Staged rollout with canary deployments. We configure monitoring, alerting, and on-call runbooks. Your team gets full documentation and training.

Deliverables: Deployed system, monitoring dashboards, runbooks, team training session
05

Monitor, Retrain & Improve

Ongoing

Post-launch, we track model performance, detect drift, manage retraining cycles, and iterate on features. Optional retainer or knowledge transfer to your team.

Deliverables: Monthly performance reports, drift alerts, model updates, governance documentation

Trust & Security

Designed With
Compliance in Mind

We're not yet certified, but we engineer with compliance from the start—aligning our practices with industry standards so you're ready when it's time to certify.
